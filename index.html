I've begun my journey into the world of tech and this page will be a portfolio of my skills as I develop.

  • Admiral and _Nology

    Our course is a collaboration between Admiral and _nology to take 9 members of staff from the operational areas of the business and begin to upskill us to begin an exciting journey into the world of tech!

    Our course is a 4 week journey that will take us through the basics of creating a repository on GitHub to creating our own websites using HTML,CSS and JavaScript. We'll also be covering the basic principles of Agile and how we can then best apply these in our new work enviroments.

  • HTML

    Our first days were spent learning the fundamentals of HTML, from creating our first words and sentence 'Hello World' to basic web pages with multiple sections and variables.

  • CSS

    We then moved onto Cascading Style Sheets(CSS), CSS is a style sheet language used for describing the presentation of a document written in a markup language such as HTML.

  • Bootstrap

    Towards the end of our first week we were then introduced to Bootstrap! If you look at the Wikipedia defintion you'll see 'Bootstrap is a free and open-source CSS framework directed at responsive, mobile-first front-end web development. It contains CSS- and (optionally) JavaScript-based design templates for typography, forms, buttons, navigation, and other interface components.'

    I think it's fair to say the group summarised it better with 'Why didnt we learn this first!'

  • Agile Methodology

    After an exciting first week of coding HTML and CSS, we then moved into a block of theory work around Agile. Agile is an iterative approach to project management and software development that helps teams deliver value to their customers faster and with fewer headaches.Requirements, plans, and results are evaluated continuously so teams have a natural mechanism for responding to change quickly.

  • Trello and Requirements Gathering

    We've also created and managed our first product backlog for 'Health Wealth'. We were tasked with gathering requirments from a very unsure customer(Gerrard.. Ollie) for an app to log and track a users illness and symptoms.     After the slightly more theory heavy section covering Agile, Javascript was certainly an eye opening welcome back to the wonderful world of coding! JavaScript is a text-based programming language used both on the client-side and server-side that allows you to make web pages interactive.
